About me


My name is Omri Arad,and since I was a little boy I had this obsession about space and everything connected to it. Growing up I watched shows about space,read A lot of sci-fi books and even looked at the moon through my uncle’s telescope. nowadays i usually watch documentaries and read articles.

A couple of years ago i discovered a video game named “Elite Dangerous” which i play with a “hotas”(hands on throttle-and-stick) that lets you fly a spaceship in a 1 on 1 scale of our galaxy “the milky way”. i'm just having a blast visiting our solar system,checking out all the wired(and cool!) stuff we have in our universe such as:black holes,neutron stars,pulsars and what not.

Anyway,I am really excited about the James Webb telescope,I think it's going to expand our knowledge about our universe by a tenfold! and of course provide us with some stunning pictures.


I can't wait for it to start working.
